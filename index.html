Welcome to Santiniketan Tribal Academy

Santiniketan Tribal Academy is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education, healthcare, and sustainable living solutions to tribal communities. Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty and empower the next generation with the tools they need to build a better future for themselves and their communities. We believe in an inclusive and holistic approach to education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also on cultural preservation and personal development.

Through our dedicated staff and volunteers, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes traditional subjects along with vocational training and skill development workshops. We also provide health and nutrition programs, ensuring the well-being of our students. Your support helps us continue our vital work and expand our reach to more deserving communities.

About Us

Established with a dream to empower the most marginalized sections of society, the Santiniketan Tribal Academy stands as a testament to the transformative power of education. We believe that every child, regardless of their background, deserves the opportunity to learn and thrive. Our approach is rooted in community engagement, ensuring that our programs are culturally relevant and meet the specific needs of the people we serve.

Our campus is more than just a school; it's a home where students receive not only an education but also a nurturing environment, nutritious meals, and medical support. We are proud of our students' achievements and their growing confidence as they prepare to become the leaders of tomorrow.
